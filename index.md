## Why This Exists

Files can record behaviour.

But they do not always record:
- fear
- survival
- sensory overwhelm
- attachment wounds
- prolonged uncertainty
- emotional exhaustion
- the human body carrying all of it

This archive exists to explore that missing space.

---

## Core Message

The difference between distress and danger matters.

Because when distress becomes repeatedly interpreted as risk without enough understanding of trauma, autism, or nervous-system survival, the file can slowly move further away from the human being living inside it.
